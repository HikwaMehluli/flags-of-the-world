Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 22 June 2026

At Flags of the World, accessible from https://hikwamehluli.github.io/flags-of-the-world/, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Flags of the World and how we use it.

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.

What we collect

Flags of the World is an offline-first application. We collect minimal data:

  • Game Data: Information about your gameplay including moves, time, difficulty level, continent, region, and player name when you submit scores. This data is stored locally in your browser via IndexedDB.
  • Local Preferences: Theme preference (light/dark mode) stored as a cookie.
  • Technical Data: Anonymous usage data collected through Google Analytics.

What we do with the information we gather

We use the information we collect in the following ways:

  • Game Functionality: To display your local scores and game statistics.
  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with our game and improve our services through Google Analytics.
  • Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms of service.

Data Storage

All game data is stored locally on your device using IndexedDB and localStorage. No personal information is transmitted to external servers except for anonymous Google Analytics data.

You can clear your local data at any time by clearing your browser's site data.

How we use cookies

Flags of the World does not use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes. We use a single essential cookie for theme preference:

  • theme_preference: Stores your theme preference (light/dark mode) for convenience (expires after 30 days)

We do not use third-party cookies for advertising or tracking purposes.
