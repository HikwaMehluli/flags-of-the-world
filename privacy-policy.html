Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 9, 2026

At Flags of the World, accessible from https://hikwamehluli.github.io/flags-of-africa/, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Flags of the World and how we use it.

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.

What we collect

We collect the following information from users of Flags of the World:

  • Email Address: When you register for an account, we collect your email address for authentication purposes.
  • Username: A unique username chosen during account registration for identification purposes.
  • Full Name: Optionally provided by users when creating or updating their profile.
  • Country Information: Optionally provided by users when creating or updating their profile, and when submitting game scores.
  • Avatar/Profile Picture: Optionally uploaded by users for profile customization.
  • Game Data: Information about your gameplay including moves, time, difficulty level, continent, and region selected when you submit scores.
  • Local Storage Data: Game preferences, scores, and settings stored locally in your browser when not logged in.
  • Technical Data: Browser type, IP address, operating system, and other technical information collected through Google Analytics.

What we do with the information we gather

We use the information we collect in the following ways:

  • Account Management: To create and manage your user account, authenticate your identity, and provide access to your profile and game history.
  • Game Functionality: To enable global leaderboards, track your personal scores, and provide personalized gaming experiences.
  • Communication: To send you updates about the game, respond to your inquiries, and provide customer support.
  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with our game and improve our services through Google Analytics.
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraudulent activities and ensure the security of our platform.
  • Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms of service.

Security

We value your trust in providing us with your personal information, thus we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure a level of security appropriate to the risk, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit using HTTPS/TLS protocols
  • Secure authentication systems with password hashing
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Access controls limiting data access to authorized personnel only
  • Secure storage of sensitive information with industry-standard practices

However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Where is your information held

Your information is held on the servers operated by credible third parties including Supabase, Google, and Amazon Web Services, Inc. These servers are physically located in the European Union or United States of America. We ensure that these third-party service providers have adequate security measures in place and comply with applicable data protection laws.

Specifically:

  • Supabase: Hosts our authentication system and database services
  • Google Analytics: Processes analytical data about user interactions
  • Amazon Web Services: May host our static content and related services

How long do we keep your information

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide you with our services and to comply with our legal obligations. Specifically:

  • Account Information: Retained as long as your account remains active
  • Game Scores: Retained as long as your account remains active or until you request deletion
  • Local Storage Data: Retained in your browser until you clear your browser data
  • Analytics Data: Retained according to Google Analytics retention policies

If you delete your account, we will remove your personal information from our active databases within 30 days, though some information may be retained for legal compliance purposes.

How to delete your account and related information

You can delete your account and all associated information at any time by:

  • Navigating to your profile page within the application
  • Finding the "Delete Account" button (clear red button)
  • Following the confirmation prompts to verify your identity
  • Type "DELETE" in the confirmation field and proceed

Upon account deletion:

  • All personal information will be removed from our active databases
  • All game scores associated with your account will be deleted
  • Your profile information will be removed
  • You will be logged out of the application
  • Your account will be deactivated in our authentication system

If you have any issues deleting your account through the application, please contact us at the information provided below.

How we use cookies

Flags of the World does not use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes. However, we may use temporary cookies for essential functions such as:

  • Maintaining your login session
  • Storing your game preferences temporarily
  • Enabling certain interactive features of our application

These cookies are essential for the proper functioning of our application and are automatically removed when you close your browser or log out.

List of cookies used on our website/s

Our application uses the following cookies:

  • session_token: Essential for maintaining your authenticated session (expires when you log out)
  • theme_preference: Stores your theme preference (light/dark mode) for convenience (expires after 30 days)
  • game_settings: Temporarily stores your game settings (expires when you close the browser)

We do not use third-party cookies for advertising or tracking purposes.
