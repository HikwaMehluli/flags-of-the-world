Game Rules

How to play, scoring, and tips

Objective

Flip over pairs of cards to find matching flags. Match all pairs to win the game. The fewer moves you make and the faster you finish, the better your score.

How to Play

  1. Select a continent and difficulty level, then click New Game.
  2. Click a card to flip it and reveal the flag.
  3. Click a second card to try and find its match.
  4. If the flags match, both cards stay face-up. If not, they flip back after a short delay.
  5. Match all pairs to win. Enter your name and country when the win modal appears to save your score.

Difficulty Levels

  • Easy — 6 pairs (12 cards). Minimum moves to win: 6.
  • Medium — 8 pairs (16 cards). Minimum moves to win: 8.
  • Hard — 10 pairs (20 cards). Minimum moves to win: 10.

Harder difficulties have more cards to remember, making the game more challenging.

Scoring

Scores are ranked by two factors, in order of importance:

  1. Fewest moves — The primary ranking. Fewer moves = better score.
  2. Fastest time — If two players have the same number of moves, the faster time wins.

Each game's score is saved locally on your device using IndexedDB. The Scores page shows the top 100 scores per continent. You can filter by player name using the dropdown, or clear scores with the provided buttons.

Combo System

When you match cards consecutively without a miss, you build a combo streak. Each successful match in a row increases your combo counter. A miss resets it back to zero.

Personal Best

Each time you finish a game, your score is compared to your previous best on that continent and difficulty. If you beat your personal best, you'll see a celebration with a gold badge, confetti, and a toast notification.

Where Your Data Lives

All game data stays on your device — nothing is sent to a server.

  • Scores — Stored in IndexedDB (FlagsofTheWorld database). Kept forever unless you clear them.
  • Theme — Stored in localStorage.

Works on Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge — desktop and mobile. All data is private and offline-only.
